French Toast

French Toast is a classic breakfast item. Made from Toast, Cinnamon, and Egg/s.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs, beaten.
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon of milk.
  • 1 teapsoon ground cinnamon.
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  • 6 slices bread.

Steps:

  1. Mix beaten eggs, sugar, milk, cinnamon, and salt in a large, shallow bowl.
  2. Dip bread slices into egg mixture to coat.
  3. Heat skillet over medium heat. Cook bread in hot skillet until browned completely. 2-3 mins per side.
  4. Serve while hot, and enjoy.
