Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled Eggs is an incredibly simple dish describing the method of cooking eggs, with a little seasoning.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sliced Cheddar cheese
  • 2 thin slices ham (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon butter

Steps:

  1. Whisk eggs together in small bowl until smooth.
  2. Mix in cheddar cheese, ham, and heavy cream.
  3. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist (3-5 mins.).
