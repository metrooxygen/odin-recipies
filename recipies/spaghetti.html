Spaghetti

Spaghetti is an Italian dish made primarily from long thing strands of pasta, and tomato sauce, among other things.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of spaghetti noodles
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can of tomato paste
  • 2 (10.75 ounce) cans of condensed tomato soup
  • 2 1/2 cups of water

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente.
  3. Drain the water away.
  4. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef with onion until meat is brown.
  5. Drain again.
  6. Return meat mixture to pan with tomato paste, tomato soup + water.
  7. Cook, sitrring, until heating through and thickened (5-15 mins.).
  8. Lastly, serve over cooked pasta.
